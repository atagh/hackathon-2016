Judging Criteria

There will be two rounds of judging. A first round of judging will be used to filter the set of projects down to the final 10. The final round will be used to select the winners!


First Round

All teams will create a video recording of their presentation and upload it to mediacore no later than 12:00PM PST using these instructions!

The judges will use pairwise comparison implemented in the gavel application to evaluate all project submissions.

The top 10 projects will advance to the final round!


Final Round

Final projects will be judged by the judges below based on the criteria below and weighted accordingly.

  • 40% - Crowd Favorite
  • 30% - Wow Factor
  • 30% - Innovation
